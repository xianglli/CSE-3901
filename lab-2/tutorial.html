Tutorial of Set Game

Hi！Welcome to the Set Card Game design by Future Tech Corp! We know your are new to this Game. Don't worry！We will explain how to play this game in detail.

In the game, certain combinations of three cards are said to make up a set. For each one of the four categories of features — color, number, shape, and shading — the three cards must display that feature as
    a) either all the same, or
    b) all different.
